GUIDELINE — SAR NARRATIVE SENTENCE STARTERS AND WRITING PATTERNS
Source: Internal Compliance Writing Reference
---
PARAGRAPH 1 — BACKGROUND AND FILING CONTEXT

Approved opening sentences:
The filing institution is submitting this Suspicious Activity Report in respect of a [account type] account held by [customer profile] at this institution.
The filing institution files this Suspicious Activity Report concerning a [account type] account flagged by the institution's automated transaction monitoring system.
This Suspicious Activity Report is submitted by the filing institution following a review of anomalous transaction activity identified in a [account type] account during the monitoring period.
The filing institution has determined, following a preliminary investigation, that transaction activity observed in a [account type] account during the period under review meets the criteria for SAR submission under PMLA Section 12.

Approved context phrases:
...was identified through the institution's rule-based transaction surveillance system...
...was flagged following the detection of anomalous inbound and outbound transfer activity...
...the account was identified as exhibiting transaction patterns inconsistent with the account holder's declared profile...
...the institution's automated monitoring system generated an alert for potential [typology] activity...
...the review was initiated following a [typology] alert generated during a [N]-day monitoring period...

---
PARAGRAPH 2 — TRANSACTION SUMMARY

Approved transaction description phrases:
During the monitoring period, the account received [N] inbound fund transfers aggregating to INR [amount].
The account received [N] separate [cash/wire/NEFT/RTGS] transfers from [N] distinct [domestic/international] sources, with a combined value of INR [amount].
Within [N] hours/days of each inbound receipt, the account holder initiated corresponding outbound [wire/NEFT/IMPS] transfers directing the consolidated funds to [destination].
The residual account balance returned to near zero following each completed outbound cycle, indicating that the account was being used as a pass-through conduit rather than for legitimate accumulation.
Each inbound transfer was followed within [N] hours by a corresponding outbound remittance of equivalent or near-equivalent value.
The outbound transfers were directed exclusively to beneficiary accounts held at financial institutions in [country], a jurisdiction designated as high-risk by the Financial Action Task Force.

Prohibited phrases — never use:
"Total inbound:" (data label — not prose)
"Transaction count:" (data label — not prose)
"Residual balance: INR None" (null value — omit sentence)
"As per the data provided..." (meta-reference)
"Based on the above..." (hollow transition)

PARAGRAPH 3 — STATISTICAL DEVIATION AND TYPOLOGY INDICATORS

Approved deviation citation phrases:
The observed transaction volume represents a [X]% increase over the account holder's documented twelve-month average monthly deposits.
The total value transited during the monitoring period exceeds the account holder's declared monthly income by a factor of [N].
The average individual transaction amount of INR [X] fell consistently below the RBI mandatory reporting threshold of INR [threshold], a pattern characteristic of deliberate threshold avoidance.
Each of the [N] transactions in the monitoring period was individually sized between INR [lower] and INR [upper], placing all transactions within the [X] to [Y] percent band of the reporting threshold.
The account holder's declared [occupation/profile] would suggest a maximum expected transaction volume of INR [X] per month; the observed activity exceeds this ceiling by [N] times.
The transaction count of [N] over [days] days represents a per-day velocity of [X] transactions, exceeding the institution's velocity threshold of [threshold] transactions per day.

PARAGRAPH 4 — TYPOLOGY AND RULE RATIONALE

Approved typology reference phrases:
The fund flow pattern is consistent with the recognised AML typology of [typology name].
The observed pattern is consistent with the [typology] sub-typology of [specific variant], wherein [mechanism description].
The pattern is indicative of a coordinated effort to [placement/layering/integration mechanism] and to defeat automated audit trail mechanisms.
The involvement of a FATF-designated high-risk jurisdiction as the [sole/primary] destination for outbound transfers is a recognised indicator of cross-border layering.
The systematic sub-threshold transaction sizing across [N] transactions demonstrates a level of operational planning inconsistent with routine [financial/business/personal] activity.
The following AML rules were triggered during the compliance review: [rule name] ([rule ID]), [rule name] ([rule ID]), and [rule name] ([rule ID]).
Rule [ID] was triggered because [specific factual reason from evidence block]. Rule [ID] was triggered because [specific factual reason from evidence block].

PARAGRAPH 5 — FILING DECISION AND ENHANCED MONITORING

Approved closing sentences:
Based on the totality of evidence assembled during this review, the filing institution has determined that the transaction activity is suspicious and does not have a discernible lawful explanation consistent with the account holder's declared profile.
The filing institution has determined that the observed activity meets the threshold for SAR submission and files this report pursuant to PMLA Section 12 and Rule 3 of the Prevention of Money Laundering (Maintenance of Records) Rules 2005.
This SAR is filed under PMLA Section 12. The account has been placed under enhanced transaction monitoring with immediate effect.
The account has been referred for enhanced due diligence review including re-verification of source of funds and beneficial ownership documentation.
All related accounts identified through network analysis have been flagged for concurrent review.
The matter has been escalated to the institution's Financial Intelligence Unit for further investigation and potential referral to the relevant law enforcement authority.
Source of funds documentation has been requested from the account holder pending completion of the enhanced due diligence process.
